POWER TIPS
🔌
Protect Appliances
Always use a surge protector. NEPA voltage fluctuations can damage fridges and TVs without warning.
💡
Switch to LED
LED bulbs use 80% less power. When light is available, make every watt count for your home.
🔋
Charge Smart
Charge devices at night or early morning when voltage is stable and demand is lower.
⚡
Unplug When Out
Unplug heavy appliances when leaving. Power surges at restoration can cause fires and damage.
